EDnevnik Back-end

Elektronski dnevnik is backend web application for creating and keeping track of students grades and management of teachers, students, parents, courses and classes.
There are four user types: admin, teacher, student and parent. Admin can create, read, update or delete teachers, students, parents, courses and classes, view logs and grade students.
Teacher can view his courses, students that attend his courses and grade them. Students can view courses that they attend to and view their grades. Parents can view their children's grades. When grade is created, student's parent is notified via email.
